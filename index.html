Privacy Policy for Dweller – Roommate Finding App

Effective Date: 18th of September 2024.

At Dweller, we value your privacy and are committed to safeguarding your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect the information you provide, including while using our camera, chat, and roommate-finding services. By using Dweller, you agree to this policy.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Identification: Full name, email, contact number, date of birth, and gender.
  • KYC Information: Government-issued ID, proof of address, and photograph for identity verification.
  • Property Information: Property photos, location, and details when listing or searching for properties.
  • Payment Information: Credit/Debit card details processed via third-party payment gateways.
  • Camera Access: Used for uploading property photos or sharing media in chat.
  • Chat Messages: We collect chat messages and related metadata to facilitate communication between users.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Providing Services: Creating accounts, verifying identity (KYC), helping you find roommates, listing properties, and processing payments.
  • Communication: Sending notifications, responding to inquiries, and sharing promotional content (opt-out available).
  • Security: Fraud detection, legal compliance, and ensuring the security of user data.

3. Sharing Your Information

  • Service Providers: We may share data with trusted third parties (payment processors, identity verification services, cloud services) bound by confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information to comply with legal obligations or prevent fraud.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger or sale, personal data may be transferred to the new entity.

4. Data Security

We implement encryption and other security measures to protect your data. While we strive for security, no system is entirely foolproof, and you are responsible for maintaining your account credentials.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as necessary for our services or legal requirements. Upon account deletion, we will securely delete or anonymize your data within 30 days, except for records required by law.

6. Your Rights and Choices

  • Access & Update: Manage your personal information through your account.
  • Data Deletion: Request the deletion of your personal data by contacting us.
  • Opt-out of Marketing: Unsubscribe from promotional emails at any time.
  • Data Portability: Request a copy of your data in an electronic format.

7. Third-Party Links

Our app may include links to third-party websites or services. This Privacy Policy does not cover those external sites.

8. Children’s Privacy

Dweller is not intended for users under 18. If we discover data collected from a minor, we will promptly delete it.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or email. Continued use of the app after changes implies your acceptance of the new policy.
